Business Ideas

Capital: Ksh 0–10,000

For business ideas that require a starting capital of Ksh 0–10,000.

  • Online Freelancing - Offer services like writing, data entry, and virtual assistance with minimal investment.
  • Homemade Snacks Business - Start making and selling snacks like crisps, popcorn, or samosas locally.

Capital: Ksh 10,000–30,000

For business ideas that require a starting capital of Ksh 10,000–30,000.

  • Mitumba (Secondhand Clothes) Business - Buy and resell quality secondhand clothes with high returns.
  • Cosmetics Shop - Start a small-scale cosmetics retail business with essential products.

Capital: Ksh 30,000–50,000

For business ideas that require a starting capital of Ksh 30,000–50,000.

  • Fast Food Kiosk - Set up a small but busy fast food joint offering popular meals.
  • Laundry Services - Offer laundry and ironing services in residential areas.

Capital: Ksh 50,000–100,000

For business ideas that require a starting capital of Ksh 50,000–100,000.

  • Cyber Café - Offer internet services, printing, and computer-related work.
  • Small Agribusiness (Poultry) - Start poultry farming for meat or eggs.

Capital: Above Ksh 100,000

For business ideas that require a starting capital of above Ksh 100,000.

  • Mini Supermarket - Open a well-stocked mini-mart serving a residential community.
  • Car Wash Business - Set up a professional car wash with modern equipment.
